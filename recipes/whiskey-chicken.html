Whiskey Chicken

Fan favorite during sporting events. You can even have this food on a stick POGGIES.

Ingredients

  • skinless, bonless chicken
  • soy sauce
  • garlic powder
  • pineapple juice
  • your strongest bourbon
  • ground black pepper
  • brown sugar

Steps

  1. Saute chicken
  2. Add sauces
  3. Add done. Enjoy!
